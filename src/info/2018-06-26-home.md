### Team Racing Firefly Specification

Inspect the [standard specification of of a team racing Firefly](https://www.ocss.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/TRFireflySpecification.pdf)\[pdf-link\]. This specification aims to assist UK schools, universities, and clubs when they are acquiring new team-racing boats. If you have any queries, use the Secretary link at the bottom of the sidebar to contact us.

### Other Sites of Interest

Oxford (visit [OUYC](http://ouyc.co.uk/)) runs an active ‘big boat’ yachting section. Cambridge (visit [CUCrC](http://www.cucrc.org/)) has a strong windsurfing section. Windsurfing at Oxford is a separate club (visit [OUWC](http://groupspaces.com/OxfordUniversityWindsurfClub/)), as is ‘big boat’ yachting at Cambridge (visit [CUYC](http://www.cuyc.org.uk/)).

The Society maintains a summary history page on [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxford_%26_Cambridge_Sailing_Society). [Historic varsity match information](https://www.ocss.org.uk/?page_id=2294) is maintained in this site’s History section. Wikipedia also maintains a good history of [team racing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Team_racing).
