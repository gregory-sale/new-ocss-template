---

Elmside Neighbours ran out winners of the Oxford Magnum 2022 held at Farmoor on 26/27 November 2022. Royal Thames White took a special 'runners-up' prize by being unbeaten in Sunday's racing.

Racing was fast and furious in blustery conditions on the Saturday, but calms prevented enough races being sailed on the Sunday for their results to count. The final outcome was therefore decided on Saturday's racing. Full results will be available for a time on the [kSail website](http://events.ksail.co.uk/2022/Magnum/).

The event was notable for many well-known faces sailing under new team names. Congratulations to Oxford and their helpers, including Keith and Jo Sammons and umpires, on organising an excellent event. Thanks also to Oxford SC for making their facilities available.
