Heartiest congratulations to Robbie King of Cambridge on being selected for the biennial BUSA American Tour 2019 in September. Robbie is an undergraduate at Emmanuel and is University Team Captain for the coming year. The tour will be a challenge to the UK team, with the Americans out to recapture the major trophies that they lost during their 2017 tour here. Watch for news of the outcome in late September!
