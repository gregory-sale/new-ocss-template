Congratulations to Sarah Treseder (Magdalen, Oxf 1988) on being awarded an OBE for her services to sport in her capacity as CEO of the RYA - in the recent Birthday Honours list.

Sarah has worked tirelessly for a good many years to raise the RYA's profile and importance. Her efforts have achieved complete success and she has also established a creative, hard-working, and friendly environment in the organisation.
