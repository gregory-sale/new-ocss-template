The Society finished runners-up to West Kirby Hawks by 1 race to 2 in a restricted Wessex Warmer 2019 held at Spinnaker SC 26/27 January 2019. Exeter was top university (5th) after a tie-break with Cambridge (6th) went in Exeter's favour.

Blustery conditions on the Sunday resulted in the 2nd stage round robin being abandoned to enable the semis and finals to be sailed. The Society met Society Exodus in a hard-fought semi final, to go through 2-1 to the final with West Kirby Hawks. Detailed results will be available for a time on [kSail](http://events.ksail.co.uk/2019/Warmer/).
