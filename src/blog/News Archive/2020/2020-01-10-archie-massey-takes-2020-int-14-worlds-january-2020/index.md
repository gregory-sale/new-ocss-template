Congratulations to Archie Massey and his crew Harvey Hillary on convincingly winning the 2020 Int 14 Worlds held in Perth, Australia 29 December 2019 -10 January 2020, from a field of 66 contestants. This is the 5th time that Archie has won the title – a record in modern times.

Katie Nurton, usually a strong contender, had an unhappy championships, suffering continuing spinnaket pole breakages and problems, and being unable to compete or finish in a good many races.

A report and photograph will be available for a time on [SailWeb](http://www.sailweb.co.uk/2020/01/10/international14-worlds-massey-and-hillary-confirm-world-title-win/).
