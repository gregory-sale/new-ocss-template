Congratulations to Graham and Julia Bailey on their victory in their Dragon Aimee in the Edinburgh Cup 2018, held at Torbay 3-6 July. They also won the Corinthian Trophy.

They have now won the Edinburgh Cup no less than four times in the last five years - an outstanding performance. A full report of the event is on the [Y&Y website](https://www.yachtsandyachting.com/news/207387/Dragon-Edinburgh-Cup-at-Torbay-overall).
