Congratulations to Fiona Hampshire (St Catharines 2009) on becoming Amateur Sailor of Year 2018 on 11 January at the London Boat Show! This is the second year running that a CUCrC lady has filled the spot - Katie Nurton won the award in 2017. Many thanks to all those members who voted for Fiona.
