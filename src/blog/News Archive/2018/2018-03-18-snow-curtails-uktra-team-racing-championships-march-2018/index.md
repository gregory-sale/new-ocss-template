date: 2018-03-18

After a day of good racing on the Saturday, overnight snow coupled with bad weather forced the abandonment of the UKTRA 2018 Team Racing Championships held at Spinnaker SC 17/18 March 2018.

The Society team had an uncharacteristically off day - ending up in the Bronze League. With results being decided on the round robin races, much tie-breaking was required amongst the 24 contestants.

The Society finished =17th (along with 3 others). West Kirby Hawks were the winners, with Birdham Bandits runners-up.
