It is great regret that we note the death of John Marsh on 10 October 2021 at the age of 90. He was a Sailing Blue at Cambridge in 1954 and an active member of the Society for many years. He competed in the International 14 Class and spent three years crewing for Stewart Morris. In his later years he raced Swallows at Itchenor and cruised extensively in his Rival 36.
