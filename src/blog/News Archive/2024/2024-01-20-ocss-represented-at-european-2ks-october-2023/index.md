The Society were represented at two 2K Team Racing events in October 2023.

At the Naples event, the Society team won the round robin, before unfortunately losing the final. A week later in light winds in Berlin, the Society team finished 3rd in the round robin, narrowly missing the final.

Further details can be found on the 2k Team Racing Website - [Naples](https://2kteamracing.com/yacht-club-costa-smeralda-win-naples-2k/) and [Berlin](https://2kteamracing.com/berlin-cup-dmtra-triumphs/).
