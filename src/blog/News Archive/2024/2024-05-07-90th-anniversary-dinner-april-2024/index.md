The Princess of Wales Pub in Primrose Hill hosted the Society's 90th Anniversary Dinner on the last Friday of April. Following feedback over recent years, this was a more relaxed setting than previous dinners which was well received by those attending.

The full spectrum of society members were represented, down to the most recent graduates. A great time was had by all, with merry conversation and a number of good speeches most notably from Secretary Greg Sale, and Port Officer Alex Baxter on the Society port we all shared (although Alex would admit he cannot remember giving this speech).
