It is with great sadness that we note the death of Lionel Cartwright come time ago - on 5 May 2020. Lionel was an Oxford Blue in the mid-1970's, being Captain of Sailing and also sailing for BUSA. He was a keen supporter of the Society and was well-known in the medical field. He spent his later life in Dorset, where he much enjoyed sailing his Cornish Shrimper out of Poole.
