date: 2022-05-09
tags: ["News Archive"]

The Society finished 4th in the Swiss League stage of the 2022 Wilson Trophy, but made an exit in the quarter finals to the Seneca Motor Boat Club - one of 4 US teams in the 32-entry competition. Congratulations to the team on an excellent performance in a highly-competitive and star-studded event.

The final was an all-US affair, with the Eastern Yacht Club convincingly defeating the East Cost Drifters. The semi finals were also an all-US affair, so British team racing needs to raise its game!
